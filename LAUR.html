Welcome to the world of LAUR. I believe that games are more than just mechanics and code—they are living, breathing experiences. As a developer and designer, my mission is to craft digital landscapes that invite players to explore, feel, and connect. From the first line of code to the final polish of the UI, I build games that stay with you long after the screen goes dark.

Games Created

SORT IT OUT!

3D Survival Adventure & Puzzle. Capstone project developed in Unity.

Dive deep. Clean smart. Save the city. Step into the boots of Nylo, a dedicated sewerian, in this 3D adventure and simulation game. Explore the city’s underground sewers, complete cleaning challenges, and learn the importance of proper waste segregation. Fun, educational, and hands-on—master the art of keeping the city clean!
Mendville

2D Educational Game. Commissioned project featuring custom 2D assets.

Breed brains, not just genes! Step into the shoes of a young apprentice in this educational adventure! Take on the role of a scientist helping your village overcome material shortages by using the Punnett Square Machine to breed genes correctly. Learn genetics, solve challenges, and make science fun for young minds!
SALDANG: The Lost Soul of Bicol

Award-winning 2D Roguelike for Mythic Bicol Game Jam 2025.

Enter the ever-changing world of Ibalon in Saldang: The Lost Soul of Bicol, a 2D roguelike packed with relentless combat and mythical enemies. Explore procedurally shifting maps, master your skills, and fight powerful foes inspired by local legend. Survive the journey, defeat the final boss, and restore the Lost Soul of Bicol.

BLACKOUT TRIGGER

Switch, shoot, and survive! This 2D platformer challenges you to flip between black and white worlds to navigate levels and defeat enemies. Face off against powerful bosses in a final showdown to finish the game. Master the switch—master the challenge!
MR. KUPIDO

Platformer/Shooter. Top 19 Semi-finalist in YCC2 National Competition.

Mr. Kupido is a 2D shooting platformer where you play as Kupido, the Angel of Love. Battle through chaotic stages using enchanted arrows to defeat obstacles and match destined couples. Fast-paced action, precise aim, and clever matchmaking turn every shot into a chance for true love.
CLICK MY BALLS

PRACTICE YOUR AIM TO THIS CASUAL 2D GAME

Sharpen your aim and test your reflexes in this high-speed 2D clicking challenge! Your goal is simple: click the targets as they bounce across the screen, but whatever you do—don’t touch the black ones. As the speed ramps up and the balls bounce faster, one wrong move ends it all. How long can you survive the chaos?
HOOP DASH

Keep the ball bouncing in HoopDash! In this fast-paced 2D challenge, you must paddle the ball upward to stay in the game. Avoid the red line at the bottom at all costs—one wrong move and the game is over. Simple to play, but hard to master!
SIT HAPPENS

Flip the can to win! In this casual 3D physics game, two players face off to see who can stay alive the longest. Featuring both PvP and Player vs. CPU modes, it’s a battle of skill and timing. Be the last one standing to claim victory!
